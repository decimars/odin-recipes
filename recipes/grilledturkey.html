Grilled Turkey Club

Description

This lunch will make you never want to go back to cold sandwiches

Ingredients

  • Sliced Turkey
  • Cooked bacon
  • Colby-Jack
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayo
  • Artisan Bread

Steps

  1. Apply a thin layer of mayo to both sides of the bread
  2. In a pan on medium heat, place one slice of bread mayo side down into pan
  3. Place cheese onto bread, add two slices of cooked bacon and 3-4 slices of turkey
  4. Place second slice of bread on top mayo side up
  5. Cook until crispy and flip to cook other side to the same
  6. Remove uncheesed bread slice and apply mayo
  7. On top of turkey pace your lettuce and tomato, then replace bread
