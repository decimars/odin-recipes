Pork Tenderloin

Description

Succulent Pork Tenderloin.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs cut of Pork Tenderloin
  • Seasoning
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Season and set out tenderloin 1 hour before cooking
  2. In a large pan add a small layer of olive oil on medium-high heat
  3. Fry each side for 2 1/2 minutes to create a nice sear
  4. Remove from pan and place on baking sheet
  5. Place in oven that has been preheated to 375 until internal tempature reaches 155
  6. Let rest for 5 minutes and then cut into 1/2 slices
  7. Serves with favorite side dishes
