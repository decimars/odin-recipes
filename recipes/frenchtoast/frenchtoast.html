French Toast

Description

This simple French Toast will be so good you won't even need syrup.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cinnamon
  • A Sturdy Bread

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup milk to a large mixing bowl
  2. Add 2 eggs
  3. Add 1 cap full of vanilla extract
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of sugar
  5. Whisk mix until eggs are fully seperated but not fully blended with milk
  6. Lightly coat the top of mix with cinnamon, don't stir, repeat for each slice as necessary
  7. Fully soak bread in mix
  8. Fry in skillet on medium heat for 2-3 minutes each side until done
